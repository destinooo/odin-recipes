Lasagna Recipe

Crisp and Juicy Italian Lasagna

To make a delicious lasagna, start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a pan, cook ground beef or sausage with chopped onions and garlic until browned, then add a jar of marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, an egg, and some fresh parsley. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then layer in a baking dish: start with a thin layer of sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of sauce and cheese on top. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
