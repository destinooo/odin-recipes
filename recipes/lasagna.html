Lasagna Recipe

Crisp and Juicy Italian Lasagna

This classic lasagna is a hearty and flavorful dish made with layers of rich marinara sauce, seasoned ground beef, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all between tender lasagna noodles. Topped with a generous sprinkle of Parmesan, it's the perfect comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients List:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Meat: In a large pan, cook the ground beef (or sausage) with chopped onion and minced garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  3. Add Sauce: Stir in the marinara sauce, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Prepare Ricotta Mixture: In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and chopped parsley. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Cook the Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Assemble the Lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Layer 3 lasagna noodles on top. Add a layer of ricotta mixture, followed by a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  7. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna sit for about 10 minutes before serving to make slicing easier. Enjoy!
